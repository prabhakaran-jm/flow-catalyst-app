# Feature Graphic Spec (Play Store)

## Dimensions
- **1024 × 500 px**
- JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)

## Design Guidelines

1. **Focal point:** Keep key content (logo, tagline) in the center 80% of the image.
2. **Cutoff zones:** Avoid placing essential text or logo in the outer ~10% on each side—some displays crop edges.
3. **Colors:** Use app brand colors for consistency:
   - Primary: `#6366F1` (indigo)
   - Background: `#FFFFFF` or light `#F0F5FF`
4. **Content ideas:**
   - App name: "Flow Catalyst"
   - Tagline: "Transform Advice into Action"
   - Subline: "Minimalist AI coaching in your pocket"

## Tools
- Figma, Canva, or Photoshop
- Export at exactly 1024×500 px
