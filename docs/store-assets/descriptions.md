**Flow Catalyst** turns advice into action. Pick a coach, add context, and get focused guidance in seconds—no endless chat, no fluff.

## Features

• **5 built-in coaches** – Hook, Outline, Block Breaker, Clarity, Decision  
• **AI Magic Wand** – Refine your input before generating  
• **Custom coaches** – Build coaches tailored to your workflow (Pro)  
• **Save & library** – Keep your best outputs and revisit anytime  
• **Value levers** – Tune tone, depth, and style without writing prompts  
• **Email sign-in** – No password; 8-digit code to verify  

## Who It's For

• Writers and content creators  
• Solo entrepreneurs and small teams  
• Anyone who needs quick, actionable guidance  
• People who want AI help without endless conversation  

## Free vs Pro

**Free:** 3 runs per day, Hook, Outline, Block Breaker  
**Pro:** Unlimited runs, all 5 coaches, custom coaches, save to library
